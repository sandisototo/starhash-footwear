Welcome

The company Starhash footwear it’s a retail import and export South Africa. A founder Vuyo Khonkwane Started this business of shoes a hobby then has developed into passion. He was given a pair of shoe Dakota by his uncle who was selling shoes all over Black townships around Cape Town. It was early 90’s. That time Vuyo was a student at Bellville. When a client paid her shoes She bought from Vuyo. Vuyo gave money to his uncle Alfred Khonkwane to buy another shoe for him for to sell. Although uncle Alfred is no longer selling shoes for a long time because was robbed several times, his nephew and a founder of Starhash footwear is run his footwear business with a dream of to boost effort to prevent South Africa from becoming a dumping ground for a cheap low quality imports and support business people who’ve invested in South African’s future, and increase south Africa’s manufacturing capability. Some of nature brands John Drake, skaloni, Tego, Bishop, Tsonga, Omega, Fango, Kingstone, Carlo Berttin, Rizzolli, Claudia, Bella Afrikana, freeman and sea Port.
